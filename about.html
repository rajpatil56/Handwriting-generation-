About TextToNotes

Our Story

TextToNotes wasn't started in a boardroom; it was started in a hospital bed. My name is Raj Patil, and the idea for this platform came to me during a difficult medical emergency.

While I was hospitalized, college assignments kept piling up. I tried using existing "text-to-handwriting" tools, but they all had the same flaw: they only offered generic paper backgrounds. My college required assignments to be submitted on our specific institutional stationery with specific margins and headers.

Unable to find a tool that allowed me to upload my own paper and maintain the authenticity required by my professors, I decided to build it myself. What started as a personal solution for a student in crisis has now grown into a tool used by thousands.

🎯 Our Mission

"To empower students and professionals by providing the most realistic, customizable text-to-handwriting conversion tool on the web."

Why We Are Different

Most converters just change the font. We focus on the context of the writing. TextToNotes is designed to solve real academic and professional challenges:

📄 Custom Paper Upload

The core feature that started it all. Upload your actual college or office paper to ensure 100% authenticity.

✍️ Realistic AI Strokes

Our fonts are selected to mimic natural human pressure and spacing, moving away from "robotic" digital looks.

🇮🇳 Multi-Language Support

Proudly supporting English and 5 Indian languages (Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi) for diverse learners.

🔒 Privacy First

Your text and uploaded images stay in your browser. We don't store your sensitive assignment data on our servers.

Who We Serve

Whether you are a student facing a medical situation like I did, a professional wanting to add a personal touch to a letter, or a researcher organizing notes, TextToNotes is built for you.
