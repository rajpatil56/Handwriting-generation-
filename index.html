How to Improve Your Handwriting

Whether you're looking to develop beautiful handwriting or fix bad handwriting habits, understanding different types of handwriting is the first step. From elegant cursive writing to clean print, each penmanship style has its own character and appeal.

  • Practice cursive writing — Start with basic letter connections and work toward beautiful cursive handwriting paragraphs
  • Focus on consistency — Good handwriting comes from uniform letter size, spacing, and slant
  • Use proper tools — The right pen and paper combination makes a significant difference
  • Study different styles — Explore types of cursive writing and find one that feels natural
