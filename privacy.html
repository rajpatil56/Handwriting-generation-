Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

TextToNotes ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (https://www.texttonotes.site).

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our website (pages visited, time spent, etc.)
  • Contact Information: When you contact us, we collect your name, email, and message content
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and operating system
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to enhance your experience

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Track website performance and user behavior
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements

Cookie Types:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site
  • Advertisement Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to display relevant ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time on site
  • Geographic location
  • Device information
  • Traffic source

Google Analytics uses cookies and may share data with Google. For more information, visit Google Analytics Privacy.

5. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense:

  • Uses cookies to show targeted advertisements based on your browsing history
  • Does not share your personal information with advertisers
  • Allows you to opt-out of personalized ads in your Google settings

For more details, visit Google Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security and User Privacy

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Data Protection: We use industry-standard security measures (SSL/TLS encryption) to protect your information
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to personal data
  • No Selling: We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access

7. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send occasional updates about our service (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze trends and improve marketing efforts

8. Third-Party Services

Our website uses third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Collects usage data
  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN): May collect limited technical data

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-Out: Disable cookies or opt-out of analytics tracking in your browser
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers support "Do Not Track" signals, which we respect

10. Children's Privacy

TextToNotes is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of TextToNotes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@texttonotes.site

Website: https://www.texttonotes.site
